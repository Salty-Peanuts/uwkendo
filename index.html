University of Waterloo Kendo Club

Established in 1987 by Taro Ariga Sensei. Kendo means 'the way of the sword' and has evolved from 2000 years of tradition of the Japanese Samurai. The Campus Recreation Kendo Club is based on self-discipline and philosophies of Japanese culture and martial arts. A balanced exercise of mind, sword and body is emphasized.
