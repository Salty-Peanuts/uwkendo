How to join?

As a University club, you must be a student or faculty member to join. The membership fee is $50 a term.

We are currently accepting new members for Fall 2022, no experience required. We also offer trial practices as follow:

  • Saturday, Sep 24, Co-ed practice @ 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM, PAC Studio 1
  • Monday, Sep 26, Co-ed practice @ 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM, PAC Studio 3
  • Wednesday, Sep 28, Women/Non-Binary practice @ 7:00 PM - 8:30 PM, PAC Studio 3
  • Saturday, Oct 1, Co-ed practice @ 11:00 AM - 12:30 PM, PAC Studio 1

If you are interested in attending these sessions, please fill out the registration form for trial practices. The deadline is Oct 1st. If you wish to have a trial practice beyond this date, please email us. These trials are no obligation, and are a great way to try out kendo for yourself before fully registering with the club.
